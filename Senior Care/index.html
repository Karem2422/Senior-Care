Our Foundation

Built on Medical Expertise & Integrity

Founded in 2023 by Dr. Khaled Swailem, an orthopedic specialist with deep experience in Yemen’s healthcare sector. Under his leadership, Senior Care has grown into a pillar of reliability.

We partner with reputable international manufacturers and uphold strict quality standards. Guided by Dr. Swailem’s medical insight, we build long-term relationships, provide technical training, and support healthcare providers to improve patient outcomes.
